Net Buff

NetBuff is the multiplayer solution developed by Buff Buff Studios, designed to deliver simplicity, performance, and efficiency. Created for developers seeking a lightweight and intuitive alternative, NetBuff is perfect for quickly and seamlessly integrating multiplayer functionalities.

With a focus on performance, NetBuff ensures smooth gameplay even in complex scenarios, making it a reliable option for both indie games and larger projects. Our goal is to break down the barriers of multiplayer development, so you can focus on what truly matters: creating amazing games.
